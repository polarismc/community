What are established communities?

Established Communities are highlighted pages on the Polaris Communities website that enables members to easily find the biggest groups in Polaris.

How does a community become established?

All communities have the opportunity to become established when first register, or by messaging sneefli on Discord. They must be well populated and active in order to be approved.
