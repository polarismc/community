About C.H.I.R.P.

So what it C.H.I.R.P. ? Well first of all our name is an acronym: Community for Human Ideals and Reasonable Projects, is what it stands for. Our goal on Polaris will be to unite people from any kingdom or from none towards simple community goals such as farms, roads or even camps, scattered around the map, that will provide for your basic needs and for everyone. 

Joining the Chirp Coalition, as a simple contributor or as a full on member, is taking a break from the conflicts of the kingdoms and looking at the bigger picture. At the start of this season we want to be the first to supply bread, to everyone. You heard me right. As our first goal we will be building wheat farms and distributing bread to anyone who stops by our camp. 

We want to make a change and provide for the adventurers of tomorrow. Even providing billboards where you can take on quests that benefit the community. But all these dreams, all those projects can't exist without your contribution. Whether you want to be a constant in our growth by registering as a member (DM me) or an occasional helper as a contributor, you are needed.
